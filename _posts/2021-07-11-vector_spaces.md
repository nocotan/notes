**_Axiom 1.2 (Abelian group axioms)_** For any $\bm{x},\bm{y},\bm{z}\in X$,

1. $(\bm{x}+\bm{y}) + \bm{z} = \bm{x} + (\bm{y}+\bm{z})$
2. $\bm{x} + \bm{y} = \bm{y} + \bm{x}$
3. $(\forall \bm{x}\in X)(\exists \bm{0}\in X\ s.t.\ \bm{x} + \bm{0} = \bm{x})$
4. $(\forall \bm{x}\in X)(\exists (-\bm{x})\in X\ s.t.\ \bm{x} + (-\bm{x}) = \bm{0})$
